Running a one-person business in 2026 is fundamentally different from running one in 2023. The right AI stack replaces an operations assistant, a junior marketer, a customer-support agent, and a content team — for less than the cost of a single freelancer.

This is the exact stack we recommend to every solo founder we talk to.

## The Stack (Under $100/mo)

| Layer | Tool | Monthly |
|-------|------|---------|
| Thinking & writing | Claude Pro | $20 |
| Research & facts | Perplexity Pro | $20 |
| Ops automation | Lindy (starter) | $20 |
| Visuals | Canva Pro | $13 |
| Code & technical | Cursor Pro | $20 |
| **Total** | | **$93/mo** |

Under $100/month, and it will comfortably replace 15–25 hours per week of manual work.

## Layer 1: The Thinking Partner

Every solo founder needs a single AI that serves as a strategic partner — someone to think through pricing, positioning, hiring, and product decisions with.

**Claude Pro** is our pick. The long-context window means you can paste in your P&L, pitch deck, or customer feedback and get structured thinking back. The writing quality also means you can use it for everything from cold emails to legal redlines.

## Layer 2: Research That Doesn't Lie

**Perplexity Pro** handles every "what does X actually mean / how does Y work / who are my competitors" question. Citations are clickable. Hallucinations are rare. As a solo founder, you don't have a team to check your facts — Perplexity is the replacement.

## Layer 3: Ops on Autopilot

This is the layer most solopreneurs skip and then regret. **Lindy** (or any agentic operations tool in its class) handles the daily glue: scheduling, follow-ups, CRM updates, invoice nudges, inbox triage.

Set it up once. Get hours back every week.

<Callout type="tip">
The highest-leverage tool in this stack is the ops automation layer. Most founders under-invest here because it's less exciting than content AI. Don't make that mistake.
</Callout>

## Layer 4: Visuals Without a Designer

**Canva Pro** with its 2026 AI features (Magic Design, brand kit, Magic Write) covers social graphics, decks, and simple video. If you need higher-end hero images, add Midjourney later — but most solo businesses don't.

## Layer 5: Technical Work

Whether you're shipping product yourself or just fixing the occasional script, **Cursor Pro** gives you a real engineer-in-a-box. Even non-technical founders we know use Cursor to modify landing pages, fix broken integrations, and understand code their contractors wrote.

## What to Skip

- **Enterprise AI suites** — you are not an enterprise. Specialized tools always beat all-in-ones.
- **Dozens of niche "AI for solopreneurs" tools** — most are wrappers on the stack above with worse UX.
- **AI avatar video tools** — unless video is core to your business, don't bother.
- **Paid AI analytics** — Claude can analyze your spreadsheets directly.

## Upgrade Path

Once you're doing more than $30K/month in revenue, consider adding:

- **ChatGPT Plus** ($20) for a second thinking partner and image generation
- **Descript** ($24) if you publish video or podcast content
- **A custom agent** built on Claude's Agent SDK for repeatable workflows
