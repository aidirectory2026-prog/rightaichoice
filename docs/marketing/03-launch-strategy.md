# Launch Strategy — Multi-Platform Coordinated Launch

## Overview

A coordinated multi-platform launch over 2 weeks, anchored by Product Hunt as the main event. Goal: maximum concentrated attention that converts to signups and community contributions.

**Target outcomes:**
- 2,000-5,000 unique visitors during launch week
- 300-700 signups
- 50+ first community contributions (reviews, questions, discussions)
- 30+ backlinks from launch coverage

---

### Launch Day Rules
1. **Respond to EVERY Product Hunt comment** within 30 minutes. PH rewards engagement.
2. **Never ask for upvotes directly** on PH (against ToS). Ask people to "check it out and share feedback."
3. **Share genuine traction data** on social — "150 people used the stack planner in the first hour" builds momentum.
4. **Be in every channel** all day. This is a one-day sprint.
5. **Don't get discouraged** by rank. Top 5 = success. Top 10 = still very good. Any placement = visibility.

## Phase 3: Post-Launch Week

**Title:** `Show HN: Decision engine for AI tools — describe your goal, get a complete stack`

**Post text:**
```
I built RightAIChoice (https://rightaichoice.com) because finding the right AI tools is harder than it should be.

The core idea: describe what you want to accomplish, and get a recommended stack of AI tools with costs and reasoning.

Built with: Next.js 16, Supabase (Postgres + Auth), Claude API for AI recommendations, Vercel serverless.

Key technical decisions:
- Programmatic SEO: 40+ auto-generated "best tools for X" pages from structured data
- 5 automated pipelines: daily tool ingestion, data refresh, FAQ generation, editorial, tutorial discovery — all running on GitHub Actions → Vercel serverless

The database has 500+ tools, each verified and auto-refreshed every 3 days.

Would love feedback on the recommendation quality and UX. What's the first thing you'd search for?
```

**HN Rules:**
- Technical angle only. HN hates marketing speak.
- Respond to every comment thoughtfully (HN audience is critical but engaged)
- Don't ask for upvotes (instant death on HN)
- Post between 8-10 AM ET on a weekday

## Phase 4: Post-Launch Sustain (Week 3-4)

The launch spike fades. The goal is converting launch attention into sustained growth:

1. **Email capture:** Everyone who signed up during launch gets a "welcome" email sequence (see content strategy)
2. **Retarget with value:** Share community highlights, new tools added, interesting comparisons
3. **Build on the conversation:** Every PH comment, HN thread, Reddit question = content idea
4. **Double down on what worked:** If Reddit drove the most signups, increase Reddit frequency

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| PH launch day is quiet | Activate supporter network early morning. Post on all channels simultaneously. |
| HN post doesn't gain traction | Focus on the technical story. If it doesn't work, don't force it — HN is unpredictable. |
| Newsletter editors don't respond | Follow up once after 5 days. Try a different angle. Move to next newsletter on the list. |
| Traffic spike but no signups | Ensure clear CTAs on every page. Stack planner should be the hero conversion point. |
| Server overwhelmed | Vercel auto-scales. Monitor Vercel dashboard. Have a status page ready just in case. |
| Negative feedback | Engage constructively. "Great point, we're working on that" + actually improve. Never get defensive. |
