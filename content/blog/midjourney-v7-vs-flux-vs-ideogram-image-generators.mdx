The AI image generator market went from "five serious models" in 2024 to "roughly fifty" in 2026. Most don't matter. Three do: **Midjourney v7**, **Flux (Pro Ultra)**, and **Ideogram 3**. Each wins a different category cleanly.

## Quick Picks

- **Midjourney v7** — best pure aesthetic. Pick it for artistic, stylized, or cinematic work.
- **Flux Pro Ultra** — best prompt adherence and character consistency. Pick it for production pipelines.
- **Ideogram 3** — best text rendering. Pick it for posters, logos, and anything with legible words.

## Midjourney v7

Midjourney is still the model professional artists reach for when aesthetic is everything. V7 introduced style references that actually work, and the consistency across characters in a series finally crossed the threshold from "fun toy" to "usable for comics and storyboards."

Its weakness has always been the same: prompt adherence. Ask Midjourney for a specific composition and you'll get something beautiful that's mostly what you asked for.

**Price:** $10–$120/mo
**Best for:** artists, illustrators, mood boards, cinematic work

## Flux Pro Ultra

Black Forest Labs' Flux is the model that professional product and advertising workflows have standardized on. Prompt adherence is best-in-class. The Ultra tier handles fine details — jewelry, fabric, product shots — better than any other model in 2026.

Flux also leads on character consistency via the LoRA ecosystem. If you're producing a brand campaign with the same model appearing across 40 shots, Flux + a trained LoRA is the workflow.

**Price:** $0.05–$0.11 per image via API, bundled plans on various platforms
**Best for:** advertising, product, commercial pipelines

## Ideogram 3

Ideogram's signature trick is still the best in the industry: legible, accurate text inside generated images. Posters, book covers, billboards, social graphics with headlines — Ideogram does in one generation what used to take Photoshop cleanup.

V3 also closed most of the aesthetic gap with Midjourney, so it's no longer a tool you only pull out when you need text.

**Price:** $8–$48/mo
**Best for:** graphic design, marketing assets, anything with words

<Callout type="tip">
For a typical marketing workflow, you don't need to pick one. Flux for the hero image, Midjourney for mood references, Ideogram for the final poster with text. Total cost: under $50/month.
</Callout>

Midjourney gives you beauty. Flux gives you what you asked for.

## What About DALL-E and Gemini?

DALL-E 4 (inside ChatGPT) is fine for casual use and exceptional at incorporating uploaded reference images. Gemini's image generation is competitive but no longer leads in any specific category. Neither is in the professional top three in 2026.
